NIRS analyzes the absorption of near-infrared light, FTIR measures infrared light absorption and emission, NMR detects the magnetic properties of atomic nuclei, and UV-Vis measures absorption and transmission of ultraviolet and visible light. These technologies are widely used in various industries for analyzing chemical composition and molecular structure of substances.</p></p>
