<p><p>Flexible mechanical couplings are commonly used in various industries for connecting rotating shafts, allowing for misalignment and vibration absorption. There are three main types of flexible mechanical couplings: sleeve, flanged, and clamped. Sleeve couplings are simple in design and provide flexibility through a solid sleeve connecting the shafts. Flanged couplings involve flanges at each end connected by bolts, allowing for higher torque transmission. Clamped couplings utilize clamps or keys to connect shafts, providing easy installation and disassembly. Each type caters to different applications based on their unique features.</p></p>
