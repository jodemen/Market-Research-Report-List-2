<p><p>Smart ticketing systems have three main market types, including Smart Card System, Open Payment System, and Near-Field Communication System. Smart Card System uses a physical card with embedded chip to store tickets or payment information. Open Payment System allows users to use existing contactless payment methods like credit cards or mobile wallets for ticketing. Near-Field Communication System utilizes communication technology to allow smartphones or smart devices to interact with ticketing terminals for seamless transactions. These systems offer convenient and efficient ways for users to access transportation services.</p></p>
