<p><p>Unconventional gas markets consist of various types of natural gas sources, including shale gas, coalbed gas, tight gas, and others. Shale gas is extracted from shale rock formations through hydraulic fracturing techniques. Coalbed gas is found in coal seams and is released through the process of dewatering. Tight gas is trapped in impermeable reservoirs and requires hydraulic fracturing for extraction. Other unconventional gas sources include gas hydrates and biogenic gas. These markets are characterized by the use of advanced technology for extraction and production.</p></p>
