<p><p>The Hydrographic Survey System market comprises five main types of technologies: Sensing Systems, Positioning Systems, Subsea Sensors, Unmanned Vehicles and Others. Sensing Systems include equipment like echo sounders and side scan sonar to gather data on the ocean floor. Positioning Systems use GPS and other technologies for accurate navigation. Subsea Sensors monitor underwater conditions such as temperature and salinity. Unmanned Vehicles, such as drones and ROVs, are used for data collection in dangerous or hard-to-reach areas. Other technologies may include data processing software and communication systems.</p></p>
