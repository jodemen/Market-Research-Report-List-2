<p><p>Wheat flour market offers a variety of types to cater to different baking needs. Self-raising flour contains baking powder for easy leavening. Bread flour has high protein content ideal for yeast-based breads. Biscuit flour is low in protein, offering a softer texture for biscuits. Cake flour has a fine texture for light and fluffy cakes. Whole meal flour includes the bran and germ for added nutrition. Resultant flour is a byproduct of milling. Semolina is coarser and commonly used in pasta making. Each type serves a specific baking purpose in the market.</p></p>
