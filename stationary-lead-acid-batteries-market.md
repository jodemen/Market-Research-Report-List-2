<p><strong>The Stationary Lead Acid Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cell</li><li>Valve Regulated Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary lead acid batteries are categorized into two main market types based on the design of their containers: open cell and valve-regulated batteries. Open cell batteries have vented containers that allow for the release of hydrogen gas during charging, while valve-regulated batteries are sealed and use a valve to control gas release. Both types are commonly used for backup power in various applications such as telecommunications, data centers, and utilities, with each type offering distinct advantages and disadvantages in terms of maintenance, cost, and performance.</p></p>
