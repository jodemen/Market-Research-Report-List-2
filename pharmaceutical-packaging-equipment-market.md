<p><p>Pharmaceutical Packaging Equipment market consists of three main types: Primary Packaging Equipment, Secondary Packaging Equipment, and Labeling & Serialization Equipment. Primary packaging equipment is used for the initial packaging of drugs, such as blister packs or bottles. Secondary packaging equipment is used for grouping and labeling primary packages. Labeling & Serialization Equipment, on the other hand, is used for adding labels and serial numbers to individual drug packages for tracking and traceability purposes. These equipment types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and quality of pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
