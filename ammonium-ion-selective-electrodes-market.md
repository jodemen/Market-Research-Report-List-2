Liquid membrane electrodes use a solvent-based membrane to selectively attract ammonium ions, while PVC membrane electrodes utilize a polyvinyl chloride membrane. Both types of electrodes offer advantages in terms of sensitivity and selectivity when measuring ammonium ion concentrations, making them valuable tools in various industries such as environmental monitoring and agriculture.</p></p>
