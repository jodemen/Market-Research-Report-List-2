<p><p>Cosmetics ODM (Original Design Manufacturer) market types include ODM With Packaging Materials and ODM Without Packaging Materials. ODM With Packaging Materials involves the manufacturer providing both the cosmetic product and the packaging materials, allowing for a complete and ready-to-sell product. On the other hand, ODM Without Packaging Materials refers to the manufacturer only providing the cosmetic product itself, leaving the packaging design and materials to be sourced separately by the client. Both market types offer different levels of customization and flexibility for businesses looking to launch cosmetic products.</p></p>
