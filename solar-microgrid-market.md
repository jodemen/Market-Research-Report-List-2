<p><strong>The Solar Microgrid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grid-Tied</li><li>Independent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grid-tied solar microgrids are connected to the main utility grid, allowing for excess energy produced by the system to be sold back to the grid. This type of system relies on the main grid for backup power during times of low sunlight. Independent solar microgrids, on the other hand, operate completely off-grid and are not connected to the main utility grid. These systems require energy storage solutions, such as batteries, to store excess energy for use when sunlight is limited.</p></p>
