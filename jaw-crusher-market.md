<p><p>Jaw crushers can be classified into two main types, double toggle and single toggle. Single toggle jaw crushers are known for their simple design and reliable performance, with a single fixed jaw and a swinging jaw that moves back and forth. Double toggle jaw crushers have two swinging jaws that move closer and further apart to crush materials. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industries for crushing different types of materials.</p></p>
