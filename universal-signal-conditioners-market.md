<p><p>Universal Signal Conditioners are devices used to convert signals from sensors into a standardized format for data acquisition systems. They come in three different market types: Single Channel, Dual Channel, and Multi Channel. Single Channel signal conditioners are designed to process signals from a single sensor, while Dual Channel can handle signals from two sensors simultaneously. Multi Channel signal conditioners have the capability to process signals from multiple sensors at once, making them ideal for applications requiring the monitoring of multiple variables.</p></p>
