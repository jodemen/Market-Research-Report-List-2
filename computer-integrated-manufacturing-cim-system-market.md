<p><p>Computer Integrated Manufacturing (CIM) System consists of three main types of markets: CAD (Computer-Aided Design), CAM (Computer-Aided Manufacturing), and DM (Data Management). CAD software is used to design products in a virtual environment, CAM software translates these designs into instructions for manufacturing machinery, and DM software organizes and manages the data generated throughout the manufacturing process. These three components work together seamlessly to streamline and optimize the manufacturing process in various industries.</p></p>
