<p><ul><li>Nano Grade</li><li>Micron Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alumina CMP Slurry comes in two main types: Nano Grade and Micron Grade. Nano Grade slurry is characterized by ultra-fine particles, typically less than 100 nanometers in size, which provide superior polishing performance for advanced semiconductor devices. Micron Grade slurry, on the other hand, contains larger particles, typically in the micron range, and is used for more traditional semiconductor designs. Both types of slurries play a key role in the chemical mechanical polishing process to achieve smooth and uniform surfaces in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
