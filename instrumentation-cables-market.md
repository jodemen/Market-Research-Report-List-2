<p><p>Instrumentation cables are used to transmit signals for measurement and control purposes. There are two main types of instrumentation cables in the market: unarmored cables and armored cables. Unarmored cables are flexible and lighter, making them suitable for indoor applications where mechanical protection is not a concern. On the other hand, armored cables have an additional layer of protection, making them ideal for outdoor or harsh industrial environments where the cables are exposed to physical damage or moisture. Both types offer reliable signal transmission, but cater to different application needs.</p></p>
