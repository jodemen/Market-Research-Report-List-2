<p><p>Deck cranes are widely categorized into three main types based on their lifting capacity: below 5 tons, 5-30 tons, and above 30 tons. Cranes with a lifting capacity below 5 tons are usually used for lighter loads and smaller vessels, such as fishing boats and small yachts. Those with a capacity of 5-30 tons are commonly found on medium-sized ships for various cargo handling tasks. Cranes with a lifting capacity above 30 tons are typically used for heavy-duty lifting operations on large commercial vessels like bulk carriers and oil tankers.</p></p>
