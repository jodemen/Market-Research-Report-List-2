<p><strong>The Swing Check Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Disc Swing Check Valve</li><li>Double Disc Swing Check Valve</li><li>Many Disc Swing Check Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Swing check valves come in various types based on the number of discs they have. Single disc swing check valves are the most common and simple design, allowing flow in one direction only. Double disc swing check valves have two discs for enhanced sealing and lower pressure drop. Many disc swing check valves, also known as wafer check valves, have multiple discs to provide better sealing and efficiency in high-pressure applications. Each type offers specific advantages depending on the flow rate and pressure requirements.</p></p>
