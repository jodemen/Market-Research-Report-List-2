<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Boiler Control Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Modulating Control</li><li>On/Off Control</li><li>High-fire/low-fire Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boiler control market types include modulating control, on/off control, and high-fire/low-fire control. Modulating control adjusts the boiler's firing rate based on the heating demand, providing precise temperature control and energy efficiency. On/off control simply switches the boiler on or off based on the set point temperature. High-fire/low-fire control allows the boiler to operate at high or low firing rates depending on the heating requirements, optimizing fuel consumption and performance. Each type of control offers unique advantages for different applications in the boiler industry.</p></p>
