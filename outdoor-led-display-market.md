<p><ul><li>Surface Mounted</li><li>Individually Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Outdoor LED Display market can be categorized into two main types: Surface Mounted and Individually Mounted. Surface Mounted displays feature LED modules that are directly attached to the display surface, providing a seamless appearance. On the other hand, Individually Mounted displays have separate LED modules that are mounted individually onto the display structure, allowing for greater customization and flexibility in design. Both types of displays are widely used for outdoor advertising, digital signage, and other commercial applications.</p></p>
