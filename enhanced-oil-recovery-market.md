<p><p>Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) market consists of various types such as CO2 EOR, Nitrogen EOR, Polymer Flooding, Surfactant Flooding, Alkaline Flooding, Micellar Flooding, and others. CO2 EOR involves injecting carbon dioxide into the reservoir to increase oil recovery. Nitrogen EOR uses nitrogen gas to improve oil displacement. Polymer Flooding utilizes polymers to improve sweep efficiency. Surfactant Flooding involves using surfactants to reduce interfacial tension. Alkaline Flooding involves injecting alkaline agents to increase oil recovery. Micellar Flooding uses surfactants and alkalis to improve oil displacement. Other EOR techniques include thermal methods and chemical injection.</p></p>
