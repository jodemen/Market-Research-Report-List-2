<p><ul><li>Direct Illumination Strobes</li><li>Fiber Optic Strobes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct Illumination Strobes are a type of stroboscopic light source that emits light directly from the source itself, providing a powerful and focused lighting effect. This type of strobe is commonly used in photography and industrial inspection applications. On the other hand, Fiber Optic Strobes use fiber optic cables to deliver light from a remote source to the desired location, allowing for more flexibility in positioning and directing the light. Fiber optic strobes are often used in medical and scientific research settings for precise lighting control.</p></p>
