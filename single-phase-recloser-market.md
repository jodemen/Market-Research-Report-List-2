<p><strong>The Single Phase Recloser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Hydraulic System</li><li>Double Hydraulic System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single Phase Recloser Market has two main types - Single Hydraulic System and Double Hydraulic System. The Single Hydraulic System operates with a single hydraulic mechanism that controls the opening and closing of the recloser. On the other hand, the Double Hydraulic System consists of two separate hydraulic systems that work together to control the recloser operation. Each type has its own unique benefits and features, catering to different needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
