<p><p>The video sync separator market can be categorized into three main types: composite, horizontal, and vertical. Composite video sync separators process the entire video signal, extracting horizontal and vertical sync information. Horizontal sync separators focus on separating the horizontal sync signal from the video signal. Vertical sync separators, on the other hand, extract the vertical sync signal. Each type of sync separator plays a crucial role in ensuring proper synchronization of video signals in various display devices and systems.</p></p>
