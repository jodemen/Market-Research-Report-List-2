<p><p>Anti-corrosion bags are available in two main types: mono-layer and multi-layer. Mono-layer bags are made from a single layer of protective material, providing basic protection against corrosion. On the other hand, multi-layer bags consist of multiple layers of materials with different properties, offering enhanced protection against corrosion. While mono-layer bags are more cost-effective, multi-layer bags are recommended for storing items that are highly susceptible to corrosion, such as electronic components or machinery parts.</p></p>
