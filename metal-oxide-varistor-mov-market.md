<p><p>A Metal Oxide Varistor (MOV) is a type of voltage-dependent resistor made of zinc oxide that is commonly used to protect electrical circuits from overvoltage transients. It operates by changing its resistance in response to the applied voltage, effectively absorbing excess energy and protecting connected devices. MOVs are commonly used in surge protection devices and power supplies.</p><p>The Metal Oxide Varistor (MOV) Market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period, with a projected Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 14.5%. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for surge protection devices in various industries such as electronics, automotive, telecommunications, and industrial automation. The rise in the number of electronic devices and the growing emphasis on protecting sensitive equipment from voltage surges are also driving the market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the Metal Oxide Varistor Market include the development of advanced MOV technologies with improved energy absorption capabilities and faster response times.
