<p><ul><li>Absorption Grating</li><li>Phase Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-Ray Grating Market consists of two main types - Absorption Grating and Phase Grating. Absorption Grating works by absorbing X-rays at specific energies, allowing only certain wavelengths to pass through. Phase Grating, on the other hand, manipulates the phase of the X-rays without much absorption, resulting in enhanced contrast in the images produced. Both types play a crucial role in X-ray applications, offering different advantages and applications in various industries such as medical imaging, material science, and semiconductor testing.</p></p>
