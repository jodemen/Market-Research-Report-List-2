<p><p>Medical imaging equipment market types include X-ray devices, which use electromagnetic radiation to produce images of the body's internal structures. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) equipment utilizes magnetic fields and radio waves to generate detailed images of organs and tissues. Nuclear imaging equipment involves the use of radioactive substances to create images of the body's physiological processes. Other types of imaging equipment may include ultrasound machines, CT scanners, and mammography devices, each offering unique capabilities for diagnosing and treating medical conditions.</p></p>
