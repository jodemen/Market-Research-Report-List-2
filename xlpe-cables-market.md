<p><p>XLPE cables market can be categorized into four types based on voltage range - Low Voltage (LV), Medium Voltage (MV), High Voltage (HV), and Extra High Voltage (EHV) XLPE cables. LV XLPE cables are suitable for low voltage applications such as residential and commercial buildings. MV XLPE cables are used for distribution networks, while HV XLPE cables are for industrial and utility applications. EHV XLPE cables are designed for transmitting electricity over long distances with high voltage requirements. Each type of XLPE cable caters to different voltage needs in various sectors.</p></p>
