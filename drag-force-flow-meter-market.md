<p><p>The Drag Force Flow Meter market consists of two main types: mechanical and electronic. </p><p>Mechanical flow meters rely on physical obstructions in the flow path to create drag force, which is then measured to determine flow rate. These meters are typically used in applications where simplicity and cost-effectiveness are prioritized.</p><p>Electronic flow meters, on the other hand, use sensors and electronic circuitry to measure the drag force on the flowing fluid. These meters offer higher accuracy and precision, making them suitable for more demanding or critical applications.</p></p>
