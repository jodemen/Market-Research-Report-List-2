<p><ul><li>Single Phase Regulator Rectifier</li><li>Three Phase Regulator Rectifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Regulator rectifiers are electronic devices used in electrical systems to regulate and convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC). Single phase regulator rectifiers are designed for applications requiring power conversion from a single-phase AC input. Three phase regulator rectifiers, on the other hand, are used in systems with a three-phase AC input. Both types are important in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial equipment where stable DC power supply is essential for proper functioning of electronic devices.</p></p>
