<p><p>The water softener installation market can be broadly categorized into two types: whole-home systems and split systems. Whole-home systems treat water for the entire household by being installed directly into the plumbing system. Split systems, on the other hand, consist of separate units installed at specific water outlets, such as faucets or showers. Both types aim to reduce the hardness of water by removing minerals like calcium and magnesium, providing households with softer and more manageable water for daily use.</p></p>
