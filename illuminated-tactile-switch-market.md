<p><ul><li>Surface-Mounting</li><li>Throgh-hole Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Illuminated tactile switches are available in two main market types: surface-mounting and through-hole mounting. Surface-mounting switches are installed directly onto the surface of a circuit board, making them more compact and easier to manufacture. Through-hole mounting switches are inserted through holes in the circuit board and then soldered in place, providing a more secure connection. Both types of switches offer illuminated indicators, making them ideal for applications where visibility in low-light conditions is important.</p></p>
