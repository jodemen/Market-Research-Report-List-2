<p><p>Light duty chain hoists are commonly available in two main market types: manual chain hoists and electric chain hoists. Manual chain hoists require physical effort from the operator to lift and lower loads, making them ideal for simpler applications with lighter loads. On the other hand, electric chain hoists are powered by electricity and offer a more efficient and automated lifting solution, making them suitable for heavier loads and continuous lifting operations. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of users in the material handling industry.</p></p>
