<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electrochromic Storage Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Oxides</li><li>Conducting Polymers</li><li>Inorganic Non-Oxides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrochromic storage devices are divided into three main types based on their materials: metal oxides, conducting polymers, and inorganic non-oxides. Metal oxides, such as tungsten oxide, are commonly used in electrochromic devices due to their stability and high performance. Conducting polymers, like polyaniline, offer flexibility and ease of processing. Inorganic non-oxides, such as niobium oxide, provide superior optical properties. Each type offers unique advantages and is utilized in various applications within the electrochromic storage devices market.</p></p>
