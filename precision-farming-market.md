<p><p>Precision farming market includes three main types: Guidance System, Remote Sensing, and Variable-Rate Technology. Guidance systems provide accurate positioning data for vehicles and equipment, increasing efficiency and reducing waste. Remote sensing technology utilizes drones or satellites to gather data on crop health and soil conditions, allowing farmers to make informed decisions. Variable-rate technology enables farmers to apply inputs such as water, fertilizer, and pesticides at precise rates according to specific field conditions, optimizing yields and reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
