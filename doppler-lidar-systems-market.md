<p><strong>The Doppler LiDAR Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse</li><li>Continuous Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Doppler LiDAR Systems are classified into two main market types - Pulse and Continuous Wave. Pulse LiDAR systems emit short pulses of laser light in order to measure distance and velocity based on the Doppler effect. On the other hand, Continuous Wave LiDAR systems emit a continuous beam of laser light to provide continuous measurements of velocity. Both systems are commonly used for applications such as atmospheric studies, wind resource assessment, and traffic monitoring.</p></p>
