<p><p>Dental sintering ovens are classified into different types based on their heating mechanisms and functionalities. Muffle ovens use a chamber to heat the material evenly, vacuum ovens remove air and moisture during the sintering process, infrared ovens use infrared radiation for heating, and microwave ovens utilize microwave energy to sinter the material. Other types of sintering ovens may include combination ovens with multiple heating mechanisms. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the sintering process.</p></p>
