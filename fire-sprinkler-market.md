<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fire Sprinkler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Fire Sprinklers</li><li>Dry Fire Sprinklers</li><li>Deluge Fire Sprinklers</li><li>Preaction Fire Sprinklers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire sprinkler market can be divided into four main types based on their operation methods: Wet fire sprinklers, which are constantly filled with water and are ready to discharge immediately upon detecting a fire; Dry fire sprinklers, which hold pressurized air or nitrogen and are activated before the water supply is released; Deluge fire sprinklers, which are open and release a large volume of water simultaneously in response to a fire alarm; and Preaction fire sprinklers, which combine characteristics of both wet and dry systems for added control and prevention measures.</p></p>
