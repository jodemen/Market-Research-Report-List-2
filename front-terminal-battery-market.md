<p><ul><li>AGM Battery</li><li>GEL Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Front Terminal Battery Market includes two main types: AGM Battery and GEL Battery. AGM Battery, also known as Absorbent Glass Mat Battery, is a type of lead-acid battery where the electrolyte is held in glass mat separators. This design allows for a spill-proof and maintenance-free operation. On the other hand, GEL Battery uses silica gel to immobilize the electrolyte, making it safer and more resistant to vibration and deep discharges. Both types are commonly used in telecommunications, data centers, and UPS systems.</p></p>
