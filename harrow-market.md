<p><p>Harrow market types are varied to cater to different agricultural needs. Spring harrows are designed for light weeding and seedbed preparation. Roller harrows are used for leveling and pressing the soil. Chain harrows are effective for breaking up clods and smoothing the soil surface. Disc harrows are ideal for cutting and mixing crop residues. Other types of harrows include tine harrows, drag harrows, and spike tooth harrows, each serving a specific purpose in soil preparation and cultivation.</p></p>
