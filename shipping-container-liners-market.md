<p><p>Shipping container liners are classified into three main types:</p><p>1. PP Container Liners: These liners are made of polypropylene material, which is known for its strength and resistance to moisture. PP liners are suitable for bulk cargo transportation and storage, providing protection against contamination and external elements.</p><p>2. PE Container Liners: Polyethylene liners are durable, flexible, and have good chemical resistance. PE liners are commonly used for transporting food, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals in bulk containers.</p><p>3. Other Market: This category includes liners made from materials such as PVC, nylon, and polyester, which cater to specific industry requirements such as temperature control, static protection, and barrier properties.</p></p>
