<p><p>Toroidal mirrors are used in various applications such as X-ray imaging, beam correction, and anamorphic focusing. In X-ray imaging, toroidal mirrors help to focus and direct X-rays for detailed imaging and analysis. In beam correction, they are used to improve the quality and accuracy of laser beams. In anamorphic focusing, toroidal mirrors are used to stretch or compress laser beams in one direction, enabling precise control and manipulation of the beam's properties.</p></p>
